The Maison

Brilliance is a discipline, not an accident

Velouria was founded on a single conviction: that a jewel should hold light the way a memory holds a moment — completely, and without apology.

Every stone is chosen by hand, every setting drawn before it is cast. Our master jewellers work in silver and platinum-white to let the blue of each sapphire fall deepest at its heart.
